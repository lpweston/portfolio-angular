About Me Launie Weston (they/them) transgender Non-binary code Web developer home Warrington science Scientific education
I am a non-binary web developer, living in Warrington. As my day job I work at Waters developing the next generation of integrated web based applications used to run Waters Mass Spectroscopy and Liquid Chromatography systems.
In my spare time I'm passionate about working on projects to help local communities, particularly LGBTQ+ groups. As well as web development, I am interested in maker projects, using raspberry pi to combine my love of programming with gadgets.
When I'm not on my computer I love to spend time with family: my husband and our dog, a loveable staffy called Marvin. Between long walks, DIY and boardgames I also sometimes find time to put my feet up and relax.
